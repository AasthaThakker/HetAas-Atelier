Order Policies & FAQs

Please read our shipping, return, and creation guidelines below.

How do I place an order?
Add your desired items (either standard shop items or customized creations) to your Resin Basket. When ready, open the basket and click checkout. This copies a fully detailed order list to your clipboard and opens Instagram DM so you can paste and send it directly!
What are the payment and refund rules?
We accept pre-paid orders only. Since all items are handcrafted and customized specifically for you, no returns, refunds, or exchanges are accepted.
How long does it take to make customized items?
Resin curing is a slow, artistic process. It takes around 4 to 6 days to design, pour, bubble-pop, and fully cure a customized order before it can be collected or dispatched.
What is the shipping policy?
We do not offer free shipping. Customers in Ahmedabad can arrange a self-pickup. If you want us to ship anywhere else across India, the delivery costs must be borne entirely by the buyer.
